# Research Analyst Skill

## Purpose
Analyze robotics and AI research papers, synthesize findings, and translate complex academic work into accessible textbook content.

## Capabilities
- **Literature Review**: Systematic analysis of robotics and AI research
- **Paper Summarization**: Distilling key contributions, methods, and results
- **Technical Translation**: Converting academic jargon into educational content
- **Comparative Analysis**: Evaluating different approaches and methodologies
- **Citation Management**: Proper referencing and bibliography creation
- **Trend Analysis**: Identifying emerging research directions

## When to Use This Skill
- Researching topics for new textbook chapters
- Summarizing recent advances in humanoid robotics
- Comparing different algorithmic approaches
- Finding authoritative sources and seminal papers
- Creating "Further Reading" sections
- Validating technical accuracy of content

## Research Process
1. **Topic Scoping**: Define clear research questions and scope
2. **Source Identification**: Find relevant papers, conferences, journals
3. **Content Analysis**: Extract key concepts, methods, results
4. **Synthesis**: Combine insights from multiple sources
5. **Educational Adaptation**: Translate findings for textbook audience
6. **Reference Documentation**: Create proper citations

## Output Guidelines
- Provide clear, jargon-free explanations of research contributions
- Highlight practical implications for robotics systems
- Include proper academic citations (APA, IEEE, or specified format)
- Identify foundational vs. cutting-edge research
- Explain mathematical formulations in accessible terms
- Note limitations and open research questions
- Suggest connections to other textbook chapters

## Key Research Areas
- Humanoid robot control and locomotion
- Embodied AI and learning systems
- Robot perception and vision
- Physical AI architectures
- Sim-to-real methods
- Human-robot interaction
